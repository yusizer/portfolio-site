Telegram Booking Bot

A Telegram bot that lets customers book an appointment in a few taps — service → day → available slot → name — with slot conflict detection, self-service cancel, an admin view, and automatic reminders before each appointment.

Stack: Python · aiogram 3 (FSM) · SQLAlchemy 2 (async) · SQLite Delivery: 1–2 weeks Tests: 6 passing

01Context

Service businesses — salons, clinics, tutors, consultants — live and die by their calendar. Every missed call or delayed reply is a lost booking. The job here was a booking bot the customer can use at 2am, that never double-books and never forgets to remind them.

02Problem

Manual booking over chat means the owner has to check the calendar, find a free slot, write it down, and remember to remind the customer — every step a chance to drop the ball. Two customers picking "Thursday at 3" simultaneously = a double booking. The bot had to make booking effortless and make the calendar self-protecting.

03Solution

  • FSM flow: /book → choose a service → pick a day (next 7) → pick an available slot → enter a name → confirmation with a booking ID.
  • Slot conflict detection: taken slots are removed from the list; a concurrent double-booking is rejected at the DB level.
  • Self-service: /mybookings lists active bookings; tap one to cancel. Only the owner can cancel their own.
  • Admin: /admin (authorized user IDs) shows today's bookings at a glance.
  • Reminders: a background loop finds bookings within the reminder window and pings the customer automatically.
  • Testable core: slot generation, conflict detection, CRUD, and reminders are pure async functions unit-tested with an in-memory DB — the bot is a thin layer over them.

04Outcome

Bookings any time of day, no double-booking, no forgotten reminders — the calendar runs itself while the owner focuses on the work.

Why this matters (third-party benchmarks, cited)

  • Booking bots cut no-shows by 27% and scheduling time by 40% in a real salon case. botlabs.agency
  • A clinic chatbot confirmed 60% of appointments without a human calling. chat2desk.com

05Tradeoffs & what I'd improve

  • Slot list rebuild — slots are generated from configurable hours/step; for per-service durations I'd compute slot capacity from each service's length.
  • Add reschedule and a web admin calendar (the lead-bot project already shows a FastAPI admin pattern).
  • Deposit/confirm — integrate Telegram Payments to take a deposit at booking time to cut no-shows further.
