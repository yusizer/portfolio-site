Security & trust

Your keys, your data, your code

The #1 fear with Telegram bots is handing sensitive access to a stranger. Here's how I keep it safe.

The bot gets only the Telegram scopes it needs — nothing more. No admin rights, no access to your chats.

Full source code + deployment access transferred on delivery. No lock-in, no "hosting hostage."
